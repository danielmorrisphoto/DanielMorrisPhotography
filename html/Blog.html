Living with Autism

December 20, 2016
My cousin has battles with Autism or rather everyone has no idea how to deal with it. Having all everyone go about their lives thinking that everyone responds and processes information in the same way makes things complicated for most people but even more so for Autistics. To get your mind into a situation that might help one understand lets say you arrive to a new school and arrive to class and proceeed to sit down on a vacant chair. Other students arrive and look at you from the corner of their eye with annoyance. The teacher arrives and everyone stands up and salutes. You stand up since it make sense to go along but as soon as you do everyone looks at you wide eyed. The professor immediatly calls you and "What are you doing here! Why are you saluting?!". Dumbfounded and embarrased you tell him that you were just following what everyone else did becuase it seemed like the right thing to do....
